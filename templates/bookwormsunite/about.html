About the Project

Bookswormsunite is a place where readers can come and find readathons happening all over the world.

Our aim is to become the place on the World Wide Web for Readathons. We provide the tools necessary to log your reading achievements and see what other users are reading. Our long term goal is to have all Internet readathons hosted on our website.

About the Team behind Bookwormsunite

We are a team of computer science students from various degree disciplines across the School of Computing Science at the University of Glasgow.

When we are not coding up projects in Django for our Internet Technology coursework we spend our time in the Boyd Orr Building counting down the days until we graduate.
